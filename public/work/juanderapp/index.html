JuanderApp

When I worked at Stratpoint Technologies, Inc as a Creative Design Consultant, I got my first solo design project with JuanderApp. Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) need updates from the Philippines so they can still see home from afar. JuanderApp delivers fresh news, sports updates, the latest gossips from celebrities, innovative Pinoy apps, and Pinoy News from SMART (PLDT Global was the client of this project).

Focusing on simplicity and modern design, I crafted the web and mobile interface that's easy to navigate so OFWs can frequently and conveniently use the app. OFWs can easily view updates on the web as well as on their phones. The results are two experiences for overseas Filipinos to use whenever and wherever they are in the world.
